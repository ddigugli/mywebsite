About Me

Biography

Hello! My name is Drew DiGuglielmo and I am currently a student at the University of Notre Dame studying Computer Science. I was born and raised in Westchester, New York where I attended John Jay High School in Cross River, New York and spent much of my life painting shoes, playing sports, and babysitting.

I played soccer, lacrosse, and skiing. I only took up freestyle skiing for a few seasons, and now do the sport when I have freetime. Lacrosse was more for fun, I participated from 5th to 8th grade and then again my senior year of high school. Soccer has been part of life since I was three years old. I was recognized my sophomore, junior, and senior year with awards in All-League Honorable Mention, All-League, and All-Section.

Here at Notre Dame I spend most of my time with my ROTC responsibilities. I was recently accepted into the Cyber Warfare Summer Training, which is a highly competitive and difficult program to be accepted into. This training, upon leaving a good impression, will help me with my service selection senior year. I hope to commission into this community where I will be trained and become an expert in analyzing, processing, and reporting real-time signal intelligence.

Thanks for reading!
